Official Warranty & Support

Enjoy peace of mind with Apple's official warranty, covering manufacturing defects and eligible repairs. Our warranty ensures that your iPhone is protected against unexpected issues, allowing you to use your device with confidence.

  • Certified Apple warranty for all purchases.
  • Coverage for hardware defects and technical issues.
  • Authorized repair and replacement services.

Our official warranty covers all iPhones purchased through Apple and authorized resellers. If your device encounters eligible hardware issues, you can have it repaired or replaced at no additional cost within the warranty period. This warranty does not cover accidental damage, but AppleCare+ can extend coverage to include accidental repairs.

Customers can visit any Apple Authorized Service Provider or Apple Store to claim warranty services. You can also check your warranty status online using your device's serial number. For additional protection, AppleCare+ offers extended warranty benefits, including priority support and discounted repair fees.
